Une vie dansée

Give your body a function that goes beyond mere beauty. Understand your emotions, your relationship with the body and the way you express yourself. In this documentary we follow a woman whose looks are considered angelic, sweet and happy, will use dance to let herself hear who she really is.

Sueño con mi cuerpo

La suavidad del mar, los colores de Loïs Fuller, la luz que atraviesa las hojas. Todos se mueven a un ritmo de caricias.

Tattoo

This video illustrates the work of a tattoo artist friend on a major project. I had complete freedom on this project. I was free to choose the composition of the shots, the camera movements and the logo animation. This work allowed me to deal with a completely different aspect than the short film, namely a more documentary video on the beginning of this tattoo project.

Eldorado cinema

3D realization, according to plans, of an art cinema that I cherish. The Eldorado, in Dijon, which atmosphere rocked my youth.
